About Me

Howdy! My name is Jason Lin (LinkedIn) (Google Scholar).
I am currently a PhD candidate under the supervision of Prof. Jyh-Charn Liu in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University, College Station, TX.

Research Interests

My current research mainly focuses on the fundamental issues of Document Engineering and the use of Business Process Mining techniques to extract the relational properties of textual descriptions and mathematical expressions from scientific publications.
Before diving into this field, I was actively involved in a project sponsored by the Office of Naval Research, which developed a situational awareness system based on Geospatial Intelligence and conflict events to model the tactical behavior of asymmetric warfare.
Apart from the above research, I have also studied Quantum Cryptography and published several significant results in prestigious journals.
Please feel free to contact me if you need any further information.
