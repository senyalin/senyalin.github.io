About Me

Howdy! My name is Jason Lin. I am currently pursuing a PhD degree under the supervision of Dr. Jyh-Charn Liu in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University, College Station, TX.

Research Interests

My research mainly focuses on the fundamental issues of mathematical content analysis and the use of Process Mining techniques to extract the relational properties of textual descriptions and mathematical expressions from STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) documents. Before diving into this field, I was actively involved in an ONR-sponsored research that developed Situational Awareness Systems (SAS) to model the tactical behavior MECH (Monitor, Emplacement, and Control in a Halo) in asymmetric warfare based on environmental elements and conflict events. I received both my BS and MS degrees in Computer Science from Taiwan. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions regarding my research works.
