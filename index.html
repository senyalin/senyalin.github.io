About Me

Howdy! My name is Jason Lin.
I recently received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Texas A&M University.
I am currently an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Feng Chia University.

Research Interests

My research mainly focuses on the fundamental issues of Text Mining and the use of Process Mining techniques to explore the coreference patterns of textual descriptions and mathematical expressions from scientific publications.
Before diving into this field, I was actively involved in a project sponsored by the Office of Naval Research, which developed a situational awareness system based on Geospatial Intelligence and conflict events to model the tactical behavior of asymmetric warfare.
Apart from the above research, I have also studied Quantum Computing and published several significant results in prestigious journals.
Please feel free to contact me if you need any further information.
